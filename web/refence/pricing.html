Questions
answered.

If something's still unclear, reach us on Discord or X.

Why do higher tiers get longer trials? +
Because higher-tier features take time to feel. A risk calculator + price alerts are useful on day one. But whale intelligence, AI trade setups, and regime-aligned signals compound over a full market cycle. A 14-day Premium+ trial means you'll have seen at least one regime shift, multiple setups fire, and probably one whale event — enough to know it's worth the commitment.
Do I need a credit card to start a trial? +
No. All three trials start without a credit card. You'll be prompted to add payment details before your trial ends if you want to continue. If you don't add them, access drops to the free preview tier automatically — no surprise charges.
What makes the risk calculator worth paying for? +
The Basic risk calculator gives you real-time position sizing, drawdown simulation, and portfolio exposure across all 16 assets — personalised to your account size and risk tolerance. The Premium version adds correlation-aware sizing (so it knows two positions are actually the same bet), a multi-position scenario builder, and live regime context so your sizes adjust when the market shifts. Most traders save more than the subscription cost in their first bad trade they don't take.
What's the difference between Basic alerts and Premium alerts? +
Basic gives you up to 10 personalised price alerts across any of the 16 tracked assets — push notification when your level is hit. Premium unlocks unlimited alerts plus three new alert types: regime change alerts (notified when the market shifts from Risk-Off to Accumulation, for example), whale movement alerts (large wallet activity on your watched assets), and setup fire alerts (when a trade setup matching your criteria goes live). These arrive in real time, not delayed.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel from account settings at any time — you keep full access until the end of your billing period. Beta pricing is locked in for the life of your subscription; it does not carry over if you cancel and resubscribe later.
Who is Premium+ actually for? +
Active day traders who want first-hour access before all other members, small funds or prop desks needing high-volume API access (10k calls/day), and anyone who wants the ability to request custom deep-dive reports on specific assets or sectors. If you're not sure you need it — you probably want Premium first. Most retail traders are well-served by it.
